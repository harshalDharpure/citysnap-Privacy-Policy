Privacy Policy (Citysnap)

Effective date: June 2026
App name: Citysnap

This Privacy Policy explains how Citysnap (“we”, “our”, “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1) Information we collect

Account information

When you sign up or log in, we collect:

  • Name and email address (from email sign-up or Google Sign-In)
  • Password (handled by Firebase Authentication; we do not store your password ourselves)

Profile information

When you set up your profile, we collect:

  • City and locality you choose (we do not collect GPS location)

Content you create

When you use the app, we collect and store:

  • Thoughts you post
  • Comments you post
  • Photos (snaps) you upload
  • Your display name on posts and comments
  • Reaction counts (“I feel this” count)

Technical information

Firebase may collect certain device and app information for security and functionality (for example: device identifiers, push notification tokens, and IP address), according to Firebase’s services. We also use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to improve stability (no advertising ID).

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Show your local city feed and profile
  • Display your thoughts and comments in the app
  • Send notifications you opt into (feels, comments, prompts)
  • Operate moderation (reports, blocks) and prevent abuse
  • Sync content across sessions and devices
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes

3) Where your data is stored

Citysnap uses Google Firebase and Amazon Web Services:

  • Firebase Authentication for login
  • Cloud Firestore to store user profiles, thoughts, and comments
  • Amazon S3 (via our API) to store photos you upload

Your data is stored on Firebase and AWS servers and processed according to their terms.

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

We share information only:

  • With Firebase/Google and AWS as our infrastructure providers to operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

5) Data retention

We keep your account and content for as long as your account remains active or as needed to operate the app.

You can delete your account anytime from Profile — this removes your posts, comments, and profile. If you delete a single thought from your profile, it is removed from our database. (Some related data such as comments may remain unless explicitly deleted.)

6) Your choices and controls

You can:

  • Browse as a guest without an account; sign in to post and comment
  • Log out at any time
  • Delete your own thoughts from your profile
  • Delete your account from Profile settings
  • Control notification types in Settings
  • Report or block other users
  • Contact us to request deletion of your account data (see Contact section)

7) Security

We use Firebase security features and Firestore security rules to help protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure.

8) Children’s privacy

Citysnap is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9) Third-party services

Citysnap uses:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging)
  • Amazon Web Services (S3 for photo storage)
  • Google Sign-In

You can review Google/Firebase privacy information here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” when changes are made.
